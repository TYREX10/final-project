About Us

The History of PROJECT SNAPVOLT

PROJECT SNAPVOLT was born by Tyler Nubin. With a passion for gaming and community, his idea crystallized into a platform designed to unite game developers and serve as a comprehensive guide for aspiring designers. At its core, PROJECT SNAPVOLT aims to foster a supportive community where knowledge and creativity flourish. Whether nurturing newcomers or empowering seasoned developers, the focus remains on equipping every member with the tools and resources needed to thrive in the competitive world of game design. Through mentorship, collaboration, and shared learning, PROJECT SNAPVOLT seeks to pave the way for the next generation of innovative game designers, ensuring that passion and talent find their rightful place in the gaming industry's spotlight.
